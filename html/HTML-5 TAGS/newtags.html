ABOUT BANGALORE

>Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the South Indian state of Karnataka. It has a population of about 8.42 million and a metropolitan population of about 8.52 million, making it the third most populous city and fifth most populous urban agglomeration in India.
